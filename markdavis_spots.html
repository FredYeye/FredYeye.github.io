Fishing spot rating

Last edited: 2021-12-20

These are the ratings for all fishing spots. There's quite a lot of them, so I've tried to keep the list somewhat compact... and readable, I hope.

How to read this list:
Each area is marked with a number (1-31) on the image below. Each area is then divided into sub areas, which are further divided into fishing spots.
There are 5 ratings per spot, being used as follows: spring/championship - summer - fall - winter 1 - winter 2.

When entering an area the cursor always starts on sub area 1. Pressing right will go forward one step, pressing left will go back one step (it is possible to wrap around the beginning and end of the list). The same applies to the fishing spots.
