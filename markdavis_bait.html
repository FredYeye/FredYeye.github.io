Last edited: 2021-12-18

These are the ratings each bait will give when attached to the rig lures.
There are 6 ratings per bait - one for each stage of the tournament (spring - summer - fall - winter 1 - winter 2 - championship).

These values do not apply to the buzz, spinner & rubber lures. The 4 pork baits are omitted, as they cannot be attached to the rig lure.
